Privacy Policy

Overview

Texercise does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device and within Apple's secure frameworks.

This policy explains what data Texercise accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected. We are committed to full transparency.

Last updated: April 2026

Data We Access

Texercise accesses the following data types, all of which remain on your device:

Health & Fitness (via Apple HealthKit)

  • Step count — used to calculate daily activity points
  • Walking/running distance — displayed alongside step data
  • Workouts — used to calculate workout points and display history
  • Heart rate — displayed in workout detail views (read-only)

Screen Time (via Apple Family Controls & DeviceActivity)

  • Selected apps and categories — chosen by you to track and optionally restrict
  • Screen time duration — used to deduct points and temporarily block selected apps when daily limits are reached

Camera

  • Used for live exercise detection (pushups, squats, sit-ups, lunges, plank) via Apple's Vision framework
  • No video is recorded, stored, or transmitted — frames are processed in real time and immediately discarded

Notifications

  • Used to send goal reminders, screen time warnings, and blocking alerts
  • Notification permission is optional and can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings

How Data Is Used

All data accessed by Texercise is used exclusively to provide the app's core functionality:

  • Calculating your daily points balance
  • Displaying activity history and charts
  • Blocking or restricting selected apps when your points run out
  • Detecting exercise repetitions via the front camera
  • Sending timely notifications about your progress

Data is never used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the above.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Apple HealthKit — health and fitness data
  • Core Data — exercise logs and screen time entries
  • UserDefaults / Keychain — app settings and install date
  • App Groups — shared data between the main app and its extensions (remains on-device)

No data is transmitted to any external server or third party.

Data We Do NOT Collect

Texercise does not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, or any personally identifiable information. There are no accounts, no sign-ins, and no cloud sync.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

Texercise does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The app does not include features that target children, and all data remains local to the device.

Your Control

You have full control over all data and permissions:

  • HealthKit access — revoke at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy → Health
  • Camera access — revoke at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy → Camera
  • Screen Time / Family Controls — managed in iOS Settings → Screen Time
  • Notifications — revoke at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications → Texercise
  • All app data — deleted by uninstalling Texercise from your device

Changes to This Policy
